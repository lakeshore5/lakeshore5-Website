Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple builds might take 1-2 weeks, while complex marketplace projects can take 1-3 months. I always provide realistic timelines during our initial discussion.

Do you work with teams or solo?

I work both independently and as part of teams. As co-owner of Xenos Builds, I can scale projects up with additional builders when needed, or work solo for more intimate projects.

What file formats do you provide?

I typically provide schematic files, world downloads, and high-resolution screenshots. For marketplace projects, I follow specific format requirements as needed.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! I include reasonable revisions in all projects to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. Major scope changes would be discussed separately.

Can you work within specific themes or styles?

Absolutely! I have experience with medieval, modern, futuristic, steampunk, organic, and many other architectural styles. I adapt to match your vision.

What about commercial usage rights?

Commercial usage terms are discussed for each project. For marketplace collaborations, I work within established team agreements. Custom builds typically include full usage rights.
